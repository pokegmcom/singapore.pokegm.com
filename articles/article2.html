50 New Monsters Added to PokeHunt

Dear Trainers,

We are excited to announce that we've added 50 new monsters to PokeHunt! These new creatures bring the total number of monsters in the game to over 350, offering even more variety and strategic options for your team.

What's New

  • 15 New Legendary Monsters

    Discover powerful legendary monsters with unique abilities and stats. These rare creatures can be found in special locations throughout the open world.

  • 20 New Evolutionary Lines

    Explore new evolutionary paths for both new and existing monsters. Some monsters now have additional evolved forms with enhanced abilities.

  • 15 New Regional Variants

    Encounter regional variants of familiar monsters that have adapted to different environments, resulting in new types and abilities.

Where to Find Them

The new monsters can be found in various locations throughout the PokeHunt world:

Forest Areas

New grass and bug-type monsters can be found in the dense forests of the game world.

Mountain Regions

Rock and fire-type monsters inhabit the mountainous areas, including some rare legendary creatures.

Ocean Depths

Water-type monsters, including some powerful legendary sea creatures, can be found in the oceans.

Desert Wastelands

Ground and fire-type monsters thrive in the arid desert regions.

New Abilities and Moves

With the new monsters come new abilities and moves that add depth to battles:

  • Elemental Burst: A powerful move that deals damage based on the user's type
  • Adaptability: An ability that increases the power of moves that match the user's type
  • Shadow Shield: A defensive ability that reduces damage from super-effective attacks
  • Mega Evolution: Some new monsters can undergo Mega Evolution for temporary power boosts

Special Events

To celebrate the addition of these new monsters, we're hosting special events throughout January:

Monster Hunt Event

From January 15 to January 31, the spawn rates of new monsters will be increased. This is the perfect time to add these creatures to your collection!

Rewards for catching all 50 new monsters:

  • Exclusive legendary monster
  • 10,000 Gold
  • 500 Crystals
  • Special title: "Monster Collector"

We can't wait to see how you incorporate these new monsters into your battle strategies! Happy hunting, trainers!

The PokeHunt Team
