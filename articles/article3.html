How to Redeem Gift Codes

Follow these simple steps to redeem your gift codes:

  1. Open PokeHunt on your device
  2. Tap on the menu button in the top-right corner
  3. Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  4. Choose "Redeem Code" option
  5. Enter the gift code exactly as shown (case-sensitive)
  6. Tap "Redeem" to claim your rewards
  7. Check your in-game mailbox to collect your rewards

Important Notes

  • Each code can only be redeemed once per account
  • Codes are case-sensitive, so enter them exactly as shown
  • Codes have expiration dates, so redeem them before they expire
  • We regularly release new codes, so check back often for more rewards!
