PokeHunt Official Launch Announcement

Dear Trainers,

We are excited to announce that PokeHunt will officially launch on January 20, 2026 at 10:00 AM! A classic monster collection and training game that meticulously recreates the original appearances of hundreds of monsters and the core of adventure!

What You Can Experience

  • Original Content

    100% recreation of classic monster appearances, gathering companions from all sides!

  • Monster Forms

    MEGA Evolution, Gigantamax, unlocking all forms of monsters!

  • Treasure Boost

    Bond accessories activating monsters' hidden power!

  • Exploration Adventure

    Open-world map levels, discovering hidden secret areas!

  • Competitive Duel

    Single-player duels to test your strength, competing for the top rankings!
