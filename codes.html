How to Redeem Codes

Open the Game

Launch PokeHunt on your device and log in to your account.

2

Access Settings

Tap on the menu button in the top-right corner, then select "Settings" from the dropdown menu.

3

Redeem Code

Select the "Redeem Code" option from the settings menu.

Enter Code

Paste the code you copied from this page into the text field and tap "Redeem" to claim your rewards.

Your rewards will be sent to your in-game mailbox. Open the mailbox to collect them.
