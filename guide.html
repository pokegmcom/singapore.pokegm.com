Game Guide

Master the game with our comprehensive guide for trainers of all levels

Beginner's Guide

1. Getting Started

  • Download and install PokeHunt on your device
  • Create your trainer profile and customize your character
  • Choose your first starter monster from the available options
  • Complete the tutorial to learn the basics of the game

2. Exploring the World

  • Navigate through different regions using the map
  • Explore grassy areas to encounter wild monsters
  • Visit towns and cities to purchase items and heal your monsters
  • Talk to NPCs to receive quests and information

3. Capturing Monsters

  • Weaken wild monsters before attempting to capture them
  • Use different types of balls for better capture rates
  • Pay attention to the monster's HP and status conditions
  • Build a diverse team with different types of monsters

Advanced Strategies

Battle Tactics

Type Advantages

Each monster type has strengths and weaknesses against other types. Understanding these relationships is crucial for winning battles.

  • Water is strong against Fire, weak against Grass
  • Fire is strong against Grass, weak against Water
  • Grass is strong against Water, weak against Fire

Skill Combinations

Combine different skills to create powerful combos. Some skills can set up conditions that other skills can exploit for maximum damage.

Team Composition

Build a balanced team with different types to cover each other's weaknesses. Include a mix of attackers, defenders, and support monsters.

Monster Training

Leveling Up

Battle wild monsters and trainers to gain experience points. Use rare candies for quick level boosts.

  • Focus on training one monster at a time
  • Use experience share to distribute EXP to your whole team

Evolution

Most monsters evolve at specific levels or under certain conditions. Evolution usually increases a monster's stats and may change its type.

Stats and IVs

Each monster has individual values (IVs) that determine its potential. Use stat-boosting items to maximize your monster's capabilities.

Gym Challenge Guide

Rock Gym

Specializes in Rock-type monsters. Weak to Water, Grass, Steel, and Ground types.

Recommended Types:

Water Grass Steel

Fire Gym

Specializes in Fire-type monsters. Weak to Water, Ground, and Rock types.

Recommended Types:

Water Ground Rock

Water Gym

Specializes in Water-type monsters. Weak to Electric and Grass types.

Recommended Types:

Electric Grass

Grass Gym

Specializes in Grass-type monsters. Weak to Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, and Bug types.

Recommended Types:

Fire Ice Flying

Tips and Tricks

General Tips

  • Save Regularly

    Save your game often to avoid losing progress, especially before challenging gym leaders or rare monsters.

  • Use Items Wisely

    Stock up on healing items and status remedies. Use them strategically during battles.

  • Explore Everywhere

    Don't just follow the main path. Explore every corner to find hidden items and rare monsters.

Advanced Tips

  • Breeding

    Breed monsters to obtain offspring with better stats and inherited moves.

  • EV Training

    Focus on training specific stats by battling certain types of monsters to maximize your monster's potential.

  • Team Synergy

    Build a team with monsters that complement each other's strengths and cover each other's weaknesses.
