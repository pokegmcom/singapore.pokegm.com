About the Game

PokeHunt is a traditional monster collection and training game using retro pixel art style. A turn-based RPG that pays tribute to classic monster games, it reproduces the charm of traditional monster games while adding new open world exploration elements.

As a monster trainer, players explore unknown areas in the open world, capture hundreds of unique monsters, and build exclusive battle teams. Engage in exciting battles with gym leaders and legendary trainers to relive childhood memories!

Game Guide

Beginner's Guide

1. Download Now

Complete the pre-registration. You can download immediately when the game launches in February 2026.

2. Find Your First Monster

Start exploring the open world and find monsters in various regions. Touch the monsters that appear on the screen to capture them.

3. Train Your Monsters

Feed your captured monsters and accumulate experience points to evolve them. Grow them into powerful monsters.

Advanced Strategies

Utilize Type Advantages

Each monster has strengths and weaknesses against different types. Use type advantages appropriately to win turn-based battles.

Skill Combinations

Combine your monsters' skills appropriately to deal more damage. Pay attention to the cooldown time of special skills.

Team Composition

Build an exclusive team with various types of monsters. Prepare a suitable team for battles against gym leaders and legendary trainers.
